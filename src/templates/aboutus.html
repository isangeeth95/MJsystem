About Us

Majjestic Jeweler was started in 1995 by Mr. Wijji Ratnayake, as a pioneer manufacturer of fashion jewellery in Sri Lanka. Each and every item carrying the brand name was passionately crafted using precious and semi-precious gem stone and presented in an attractive fashion. With these uniqueness of fashion, Majjestic Jeweler was able to get more customer attraction. It was a novelty and Sri Lanka was moved. Exquisite jade, coral, tiger-eye, hematite, blue sapphire, ruby creations in unique designs became the hot favorite. As the brand grew in popularity, so did the variety of designs. Yet, one thing remained constant. The Majjestic Jeweler hallmark of quality. Offering timeless creations and setting trends in fashion, the brand Majjestic Jeweler ventured beyond Sri Lankan shores, to markets overseas. Today Majjestic Jeweler has an impressive global presence. Unlike any other jewellers we do not force the customer to shop and carry whatever we have in store.
